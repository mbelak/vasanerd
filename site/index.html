Seeding

What is this? Estimate which Vasaloppet start group a qualifying race result corresponds to. Select a known race for exact cutoffs, or enter a custom race and the model will predict cutoff times based on distance, elevation and winner speed. You can also use this to estimate what time you need in an upcoming race to reach a specific start group.

Estimates based on historical seeding data from ~76 races. Vasaloppet sets cutoffs manually after each race — actual placement may differ. The range column shows expected model accuracy.
